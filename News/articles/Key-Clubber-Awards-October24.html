Key Clubber Awards: October 2024

By: Zachary Ratkiewicz (Webmaster)

Updated 10:50 AM EDT, November 8, 2024

Click here to copy this article's link.

SPHS Key Club setting up the balloon arch at Mad Hatter's Tea Party (August 11, 2024)

Introduction

Zachary Ratkiewicz (Webmaster) — Hiii everynyan!! This month, we're introducing the Key Clubbers of the Month and Benton's Member! We're also giving out the award to August and September's winners (since we missed it, oopsies)!

The Key Clubber of the Month award is given to each grade's member with the most amount of points obtained and other factors.

Benton's Member is personally chosen by Mr. Benton (our club's advisor)!

Key Clubbers of the Month: August

Our Sophomore of the month is Liam Poole [11.5 pts]!
Our Junior of the month is Anna Wei [18 pts]!
Our Senior of the month is Sage Summers [19.5 pts]!

Key Clubbers of the Month: September

Our Sophomore of the month is Gwen Connors [9.5 pts]!
Our Junior of the month is Sally Rozelle [13 pts]!
Our Senior of the month is Connor Pudlak [9.5 pts]!

Benton's Member: September

Benton's Member for September is.... drumroll please.... Deanna McMullen!

Thanks to Deanna McMullen for ensuring our win in the HOCO alphabet relay!

Key Clubbers of the Month: October

Our Sophomore of the month is Paul Stoici [7 pts]!
Our Junior of the month is Matthew Pentolfe [9 pts]!
Our Senior of the month is Stephanie Gielazyn [7.5 pts]!

Benton's Member: October

Benton's Member for October is.... drumroll please.... Elise Chandler!

Thanks to Elise Chandler for taking the initiative to attend the High School Heroes Leadership Conference hosted by OneBlood!

Conclusion

Congratulations to all the Key Clubbers of the Month and Benton's Members! Thanks to Jocelyne Lioe for all the point totals and amazing stickers!!

At the end of November, we'll announce four new Key Clubbers of the Month (Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, and Senior) and one Benton's Member! Toodles!

__

Written by: Zachary Ratkiewicz (Webmaster)
