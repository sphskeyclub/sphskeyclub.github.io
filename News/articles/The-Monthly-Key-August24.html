The Monthly Key: August 2024

By: Zachary Ratkiewicz (Webmaster)

Updated 10:50 AM EDT, September 5, 2024

Click here to copy this article's link.

SPHS Key Club setting up the balloon arch at Mad Hatter's Tea Party (August 11, 2024)

Introduction

Zachary Ratkiewicz (Webmaster) — Hey Keyple!! Hope you've had a great start to the school year (yayyyyy...)! For June, July, and August, we've had a total of 627.5 service hours! Wowzers! Check out some of the things we accomplished below!

Senior Celebration

In June, we a going-away party for the 2023-2024 seniors, and it was a blast!! WE LOVE YOU <3 (please come back sometime)!!

Free Rice (NEW group code: RYHHEA3M)

WOWZA!: during our SUMMER season, we donated a total of 178,090 grains of rice! Our top 3 contributors were:
1) Tucker Henry [51,340], 2) Alison Ashley [49,950], 3) Jaiden Dodge [9,000]

WOAH!! For the month of August alone, we've donated 10,830 grains of rice! Our top 3 contributors for this month were:
1) Jaiden Dodge [4,500], 2) Anna Wei [3,000], 3) Sally Rozelle [1,510]

Thank you all to contributing to Free Rice!

Mad Hatter's Tea Party

August 11th, we had a grand 'ol time helping St. Petersburg Kiwanis with the Mad Hatter's Tea Party! There, we blew up balloons, created a balloon arch, face painted, tattooed, made crafts, and danced! See some photos of the event on our new Google Drive folder!

(!!!) Things to know for September and beyond

Last couple things! Please sign up for our Homeless Meals service opportunity (1 point; Fridays after school): the sign-up for each week goes up on the Facebook group on Tuesdays at 4 PM. Also, we really need donations: each item will get you 0.5 points!

Our Blood Drive Committee is looking for new committee members! If you're interested in being a KEY (teehee) volunteer for the blood drives this year, please fill out this form! If you have any questions, please contact Anna Hewett!

This is a great opportunity if you are looking to be the Blood Drive Committee Chair for next year or if you just like volunteering at the blood drives!

Finally, the wonderful Ava Masi created a document full of ALL point oppportunities here! Make sure to check it out!

Conclusion

I love you Keyple, continue to have a Keyful life over the year!!

Didn't get to do these fun things this month? See what you can do next month by checking out our News page and event calendar!

__

Written by: Zachary Ratkiewicz (Webmaster)
