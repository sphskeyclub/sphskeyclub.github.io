Divisional Council Meeting


Divisional Council Meetings (DCMs) are meetings of all the Key Clubs from different schools in division, where we share district information, related updates, and service opportunities for our area.

Our first DCM will be on Saturday, May 24th, at 6:00pm OVER ZOOM.  We will be discussing important district and divisional updates, hear a recap from each of our clubs, get to know each other with fun games, and so much more. In addition, we will be simultaneously holding our MANDATORY Zone E officer trainings. These are trainings that EVERY club must complete throughout the service year. At this meeting, there will also be a service opportunity, so please do not hesitate to share this link amongst your club members! Make sure to RSVP below if you plan to attend!

DCON Recap


Thank you to all the clubs that attended and congratulations to all clubs who won awards! There were distinguished board members, governor's citations, and other club awards for a few of our division 13 schools!! More than a thousand members from across the Florida District were in attendance. The following executive members were elected at the District Conference: 

Secretary: Andrea Cabrera Elguezabal
