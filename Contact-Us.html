Need a Hand?

SPHS Key Club is at the community's fingertips. You can contact SPHS Key Club by emailing us at board@sphskeyclub.org, or for more direct communication, sending a text or email to our officers or committee chairs. You can also contact us by Facebook message or by contacting us on our Remind.

To inquire about opportunities for collaborative service or to request volunteers, contact us at board@sphskeyclub.org. For special inquiries to your Lieutenant Governor (Division 13, Zone E), contact Tucker Henry at 2526division13@floridakeyclub.org.

Contact our club advisor, Roddy Benton, at bentonro@pcsb.org or bentonenglish.com. Also, don't forget to follow SPHS Key Club on Facebook to receive updates!

Yes, we did migrate from our Weebly website! However, you can still download and see it by clicking here to go to its Github repository.
