Need a Hand?

SPHS Key Club is at the community's fingertips. You can contact SPHS Key Club through club presidents Brody Whiteside and Tucker Henry through Facebook message, Remind, or by their contacts below with any questions or concerns.

To inquire about opportunities for collaborative service or to request volunteers, contact us at sphskeyboard@gmail.com. For special inquiries to your Lieutenant Governor (Division 13, Zone E), contact Anna Nguyen at 2425division13@floridakeyclub.org.

Contact our club sponsor, Roddy Benton, at bentonro@pcsb.org or bentonenglish.com. Also, don't forget to follow SPHS Key Club on Facebook to receive updates!
