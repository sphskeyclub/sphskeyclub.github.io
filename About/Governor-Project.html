Governor's Project

Lit for Life

This year's Governor's Project by Alejandro Ganaza (Florida District Governor) sets out to make sure every child is given the opportunity to foster proper literacy skills.

To leave legacies of literacy, we need your active involvement!

Abhi Sharma from India, CC BY 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

We Are In: Phase 3 (Exposure)

Lit for Life is divided into three phases: Access (May through August), Community (September through December), and Exposure (January through March).

In the months from January through March, we are in the final stage, Phase 3 (Exposure). Phase 3 aims to expose our community to literature through previously accessed books, agendas, etc.

How Can SPHS Key Club Help?

Stay updated on how SPHS Key Club is working towards Lit for Life's goal through our newsletters and other articles!

When posting about our Governor's Project, make sure to use the hashtag: #LitForLifeFLKC
